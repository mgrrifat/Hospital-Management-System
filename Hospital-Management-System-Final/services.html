Emergency

The Emergency department is 24/7 open for emergency patient. It offers the highest degree of commitment to delivering quality and efficient emergency care.

Ambulance

Regular Ambulance is available 24/7 for emergency transportation of patients maintaining the BLS (Basic Life Support) ambulance standard.

Covid Corner

Our Hospital has the most robust and comprehensive COVID care facility in the country.

Medical checkup

You want to live healthy? We have a guideline through all the meaningful service packages. Comprehensive physical exams are essential in maintaining good health.

Nursing Services

We provides 24-hour comprehensive, individualized nursing care for in-patients based on their assessed nursing needs. All our nurses are well-qualified and experienced with a commitment to patient care.

Blood Bank

Our Hospital is a place for collecting, screening, typing, processing, and storing whole blood, packed red blood cells, fresh frozen plasma and platelet concentrate, gathered as a result of blood donation, preserved for patient use in this hospital and other hospitals on request.

Pharmacy

The Pharmacy Department of our Hospital provides a comprehensive range of pharmaceutical services to all patients. Its objective is to provide patient-focused pharmaceutical care in order to achieve definite outcomes that improve patients’ quality of life.

About Hospital Management System

To serve the humanity as a whole with this noble vision the Hospital Management System started its journey in June 2018. The Hospital has agreed upon to provide health care service to the people of Bangladesh with affordable cost.
